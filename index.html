SCP-096

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures:

SCP-096 is to be contained in its cell at all times. No personnel are to view SCP-096’s face, whether directly, via video surveillance, or through photographs.

Description:

SCP-096 is a humanoid creature approximately 2.38 meters in height. The subject shows very little muscle mass, with preliminary analysis of body mass suggesting mild malnutrition. Arms are grossly out of proportion with the rest of the subject’s body, approximately 1.5 meters in length each.

SCP-096 will enter a stage of considerable emotional distress when someone views its face, whether directly or via a recording. Upon viewing, the person is to be designated SCP-096-1 and SCP-096 will enter a rage state and seek out SCP-096-1, killing them regardless of distance.
